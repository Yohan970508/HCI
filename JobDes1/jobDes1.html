Business Analyst

Company: BloomEnergy (Pvt) Ltd

Type: Full-time

location_on

Location:Colombo


Company Description

At BloomEnergy you will work in a growing, global enterprise software company where informal hierarchies promote simplicity and trust with responsibility. We strive to deepen our expertise by taking on challenging and varying work assignments in a collaborative environment together with committed colleagues. With professionalism and a strong customer focus in everything we do, from R&D to Sales and Consultancy, we are proud to serve world-class customers all over the globe with our leading products.

Acumatica is a sister company of BloomEnergy and a leading cloud ERP software that empowers businesses to unlock their potential and drive growth. Built on the world’s best cloud and mobile technology and a unique customer-centric licensing model, Acumatica delivers a suite of fully integrated business management applications, such as Financials, Supply Chain, Manufacturing, CRM, Project Management, etc. on a robust and flexible platform.

We are an equal opportunity employer and value diversity at our company.

Main Responsibilities:
  • Analysis of business scenarios and product requirements and clarify expectations with stakeholders
  • Design new features that fit the market demand and company product vision.
  • Create functional specifications, analyze backward compatibility of product changes, negotiate specifications with all stakeholders
  • Decomposition of changes in the product and setting the task for developers, coordination of implementation details and possible changes to the original requirements
  • Interaction with automated testing engineers, technical writers and support engineers within the project under development


Qualifications:
  • Relevant Degree equivalent qualifications in Supply Chain Management, Finance, Engineering, or IT
  • Experience in enterprise software design
  • Experience in Financials, CRM, or Supply Chain related ERP modules & components
  • Experience in creating functional specifications
  • Experience in ERP/CRM Modules & Components
